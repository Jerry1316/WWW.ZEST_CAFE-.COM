About Zest Cafe

Your Second Home in Srikakulam

Zest Cafe opened its doors in 2020 with a simple mission: to create a warm, inviting space where every cup of coffee tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Srikakulam, we've become the go-to spot for coffee lovers, students looking for a cozy study corner, and friends catching up over delicious food.

Our cafe is designed to be your second home — whether you're here to work on your laptop, have a meaningful conversation, or simply unwind with a book and a perfectly brewed coffee.
